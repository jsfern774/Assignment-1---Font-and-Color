Energetic Style

energetic!1!

To be energetic is to possess, exert, or display vitality and the ability to do tasks which require lots of energy.


Red and yellow are examples of energetic colors; both colors are attention grabbing and stand out due to their contrasting nature, much like an energetic person would stand out from the crowd. Both colors also relate to themes of power; red is often conveyed with dominance and yellow with electricity. Typefaces which are more jagged and handwritten help to get the theme of energy accross.
